## 1. Executive Summary

**Objetivos Específicos**:
- Mes 3: 100 early adopters (50% lifetime discount)
- Mes 6: 1,000 usuarios activos
- Mes 9: 2,500 usuarios activos
- Mes 12: 5,000 usuarios activos
- MRR al final del año 1: $50,000+

### 1.2 Posicionamiento en el Mercado

**Tagline**: "Feedback Privacy-First for Modern Websites"

**Diferenciadores Clave**:
1. Única solución verdaderamente privacy-first (sin cookies, sin tracking)
2. Implementación en 5 minutos (vs horas/días de la competencia)
3. Pricing transparente y predecible
4. API access desde plan Growth (no enterprise-only)

## 15. Análisis de Riesgos

### 15.1 Riesgos de Marketing

| Riesgo | Impacto | Probabilidad | Mitigación |
|--------|---------|-------------|------------|
| Low conversion rates | High | Medium | A/B testing, funnel optimization |
| High CAC | Medium | Medium | Focus on organic, improve LTV |
| Competitor response | Medium | High | Fast iteration, unique features |
| Brand damage | High | Low | Quality content, authentic engagement |

### 15.2 Plan de Contingencia

**If conversion rates < 3%**:
- Improve onboarding flow
- Add more value in free tier
- Increase email nurturing
- Offer time-limited discounts

**If CAC > $75**:
- Pause least effective channels
- Double down on content/SEO
- Improve referral program
- Focus on high LTV segments

**If churn > 8%**:
